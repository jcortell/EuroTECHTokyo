Technology Exchange and Collaboration Hub

BUILDING

Physical connections

Between researchers, entrepreneurs, academics, investors, and students. Via Tokyu Land Corporation's state-of-the-art center in Shibuya.

BUILDING

Virtual connections

Between researchers, entrepreneurs, academics, investors, and students. Via full-size real-time videoconference portals.

WHAT

The Technology Exchange and Collaboration Hub in Tokyo (TECH-Tokyo) is a Japanese Public Benefit NGO (non-governmental organization) dedicated to connecting the world’s top universities with leading Japanese corporations. Our aim is to foster robust collaborations through licensing, partnerships, investment into university spinoffs, and sponsored research, as well as to facilitate academic exchange and executive education.

HOW

Bringing faculty, researchers, and entrepreneurs from some of the top 10 universities in the world, and Japanese corporations, entrepreneurs, investors, and universities, closer together. Exploring academic, partnering, and commercial opportunities through a multi-year program of presentations, lectures, workshops, networking, guest speakers, competitions and more.

WHERE

Hold meetings, incubate startups, or offer lectures and presentations with simultaneous Japanese-English translation in Shibuya (Tokyo). Facilitate collaboration in real time with Japanese executives, and your faculty, entrepreneurs, and researchers using a full-size real-time video portal that we can provide for free to your university.

MORE INFO

NO COST — NO OBLIGATION

All of these services are provided for free, at no obligation, to your University or Center. We want to be your partner in Japan, and help you commercialize your technology and research so it can benefit patients, users, and companies.

Activities

TECH-Tokyo offers a full calendar of activities, all year round, with faculty from MIT, Stanford, Oxford University, Imperial College London, University of Cambridge, and more.

ALL ACTIVITIES

EXECUTIVE EDUCATION

Offer paid sessions and programs to Japanese executives, request academic exchanges, give presentations, and more. In Tokyo or online. We provide the space and Japanese translator for free, subject to availability.

CONFIRMED SPEAKERS

ENTREPRENEURSHIP & INNOVATION

We select and mentor top international entrepreneurs to present to Japanese corporates for partnerships, investment, and other opportunities. Select startups can even be incubated for free in our Shibuya space.

IP & LICENSING OPPORTUNITIES

We help match your IP portfolio to Japanese corporations' interest, and provide local presentation of your available IP in Japan, in Japanese.

RESEARCH COLLABORATION

We facilitate introductions to Japanese executives to your chosen researchers and labs for joint and sponsored research opportunities. Including fully funded Scientific Fellowship Exchange programs.

WHY

We believe that now, more than ever, it is critical for the world’s leading corporations and universities to unite, demonstrate resilience, and show solidarity in the face of the current political challenges and attacks on academic freedom. By coming together, we can strengthen our collective position and create a powerful, united front that supports academic innovation and entrepreneurship globally.
